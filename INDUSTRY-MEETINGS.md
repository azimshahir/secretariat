# Industry Meeting Types Reference

Senarai meeting/committee types by industry untuk secretariat.my persona templates.

---

## 1. Banking & Financial Services

### Board-Level
| Meeting | Description |
|---------|-------------|
| Board of Directors (BOD) | Quarterly/monthly board meetings — strategic direction, P&L, regulatory updates |
| Annual General Meeting (AGM) | Statutory yearly shareholder meeting |
| Extraordinary General Meeting (EGM) | Ad-hoc shareholder meeting for urgent resolutions |

### Governance & Risk
| Meeting | Description |
|---------|-------------|
| ALCO (Asset Liability Committee) | ALM, liquidity ratios (LCR/NSFR), interest rate risk, OPR impact |
| MRC (Management Risk Committee) | Enterprise risk, KRIs, Basel compliance, risk appetite |
| Audit Committee | Internal/external audit findings, MFRS compliance, whistleblowing |
| Nominating Committee | Board composition, independence assessment, fit & proper |
| Remuneration Committee | Director/senior management compensation, KPI alignment |
| Risk Management Committee | Credit risk, market risk, operational risk oversight |
| Compliance Committee | AML/CFT, BNM regulatory compliance, sanctions screening |

### Operational
| Meeting | Description |
|---------|-------------|
| Credit Committee | Loan approvals, credit limits, NPL review, provisioning |
| IT Steering Committee (ITSC) | Digital transformation, cybersecurity, system upgrades, RMiT compliance |
| Shariah Committee | Shariah-compliant product review (Islamic banking) |
| Tender/Procurement Committee | Vendor selection, contract approvals, procurement policy |
| Business Continuity Committee | BCP/DRP testing, crisis management |
| Sustainability/ESG Committee | ESG disclosures, green financing, climate risk |

## 2. Construction & Property

### Board-Level
| Meeting | Description |
|---------|-------------|
| Board of Directors (BOD) | Strategic direction, project portfolio review |
| AGM / EGM | Shareholder meetings (if listed) |

### Project Governance
| Meeting | Description |
|---------|-------------|
| Project Steering Committee | Overall project direction, milestone tracking, budget approval |
| Site Progress Meeting | Weekly/monthly site progress, contractor coordination, delay analysis |
| Technical Review Committee | Design review, specification approval, value engineering |
| Tender Committee | Subcontractor/vendor evaluation, bid comparison, award decisions |
| Variation Order Committee | Change order review, cost impact assessment |

### Compliance & Safety
| Meeting | Description |
|---------|-------------|
| HSE Committee (Health, Safety & Environment) | OSHA/DOSH compliance, incident reports, safety audits |
| QLASSIC Review Meeting | Quality assessment (CIDB quality standards) |
| Environmental Impact Committee | EIA compliance, DOE requirements, waste management |
| Land & Planning Committee | Land acquisition, development order, planning permits |

### Operations
| Meeting | Description |
|---------|-------------|
| Defect Liability Meeting | Post-handover defect tracking, rectification timeline |
| Joint Management Body (JMB) | Strata management, maintenance fund, common area issues |
| Cost Control Committee | Budget vs actual, cash flow, retention tracking |
| Design Coordination Meeting | Architect, engineer, consultant coordination |

## 3. Oil & Gas

### Board-Level
| Meeting | Description |
|---------|-------------|
| Board of Directors (BOD) | Strategic direction, upstream/downstream review |
| AGM / EGM | Shareholder meetings |

### Operations & Technical
| Meeting | Description |
|---------|-------------|
| Operations Review Committee | Production performance, downtime analysis, KPI review |
| Technical Review Board | Engineering design review, technology selection, MOC (Management of Change) |
| Joint Venture Operating Committee (JVOC) | JV partner coordination, work program & budget, PSC compliance |
| Drilling Committee | Well planning, rig scheduling, cost authorization |
| Turnaround Planning Committee | Plant shutdown planning, scope, budget, safety |

### Safety & Compliance
| Meeting | Description |
|---------|-------------|
| HSE Committee | OSHA/DOSH, process safety, incident investigation, PTW review |
| Major Hazard Committee | COMAH/CIMAH compliance, safety case review |
| Emergency Response Team (ERT) Meeting | Emergency drills, response protocols, equipment readiness |
| Sustainability Committee | Carbon reduction, ESG reporting, TCFD disclosure |

### Commercial
| Meeting | Description |
|---------|-------------|
| Procurement Committee | Vendor qualification, contract strategy, local content |
| Cost Control Committee | AFE tracking, budget variance, cost recovery |
| Commercial Review Meeting | Contract negotiations, claims management, partner billing |
| Decommissioning Committee | Asset retirement planning, cost estimates, regulatory compliance |

## 5. Others (General / PLCs / Cross-Industry)

### Universal Board Committees
| Meeting | Description |
|---------|-------------|
| Board of Directors (BOD) | Standard board meeting — applicable to all industries |
| AGM / EGM | Statutory shareholder meetings |
| Audit Committee | Financial oversight, internal/external audit |
| Risk Committee | Enterprise risk management, risk register review |
| Nominating Committee | Board appointments, skills matrix, succession planning |
| Remuneration Committee | Compensation framework, performance-based incentives |

### Technology & Digital
| Meeting | Description |
|---------|-------------|
| IT Steering Committee | Technology roadmap, cybersecurity, digital transformation |
| Data Governance Committee | Data privacy (PDPA), data quality, AI governance |

### People & Culture
| Meeting | Description |
|---------|-------------|
| HR Committee | Workforce planning, talent management, disciplinary |
| Disciplinary Committee | Employee misconduct, investigation findings, sanctions |
| Occupational Safety Committee | Workplace safety (OSHA 1994), accident reporting |

### Sustainability & Compliance
| Meeting | Description |
|---------|-------------|
| ESG / Sustainability Committee | Environmental, social, governance reporting |
| Compliance Committee | Regulatory compliance, policy updates, training |
| Whistleblowing Committee | Protected disclosure review, investigation oversight |

### Industry-Specific (Miscellaneous)
| Meeting | Description |
|---------|-------------|
| **Insurance** — Underwriting Committee | Risk assessment, policy approvals, reinsurance |
| **Insurance** — Claims Review Committee | Claims adjudication, fraud detection, reserves |
| **Healthcare** — Medical Advisory Committee | Clinical governance, patient safety, credentialing |
| **Healthcare** — Pharmacy & Therapeutics | Drug formulary, medication safety, clinical protocols |
| **Education** — Senate / Academic Council | Academic policy, curriculum approval, examination |
| **Education** — Faculty Board | Department governance, research grants, student affairs |
| **Telco** — Network Operations Committee | Infrastructure planning, SLA review, spectrum management |
| **Cooperative (Koperasi)** — ALK (Ahli Lembaga Koperasi) | Cooperative board, member welfare, SKM compliance |
| **REIT** — Investment Committee | Property acquisition, valuation, portfolio rebalancing |
